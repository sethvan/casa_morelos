Local Area

Casa Morelos on the bay of Matanchen , sits on 2-acres surrounded by lush tropical forest, facing the beach. This area is world famous for its estuaries and migratory birds. We are within walking distance of the world famous La Tovara National Park , featuring jungle boat rides through the mangroves. There are several other attractions, one of which is a crocodile farm.

We are also within walking distance to the beaches of Las Islitas, where the beach is lined with ramadas (restaurants). A great spot for swimming and surfing.

We are 5 minutes from San Blas, a center for marlin fishing and whale watching . We are also close to the historic site of Fort San Blas, which sits on a hill providing excellent views of the entire town and ocean. San Blas and other areas nearby also offer a great shopping experience. We hope you enjoy your stay with us!
