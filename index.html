Welcome To Casa Morelos

Our small hotel and bungalows feature two 3-bedroom apartments with kitchens, in addition to eight double rooms, one triple room and two single rooms. Our rooms are clean and comfortable, and priced to fit your budget. The facility is available for group retreats, weddings, birthdays, or any other special occasion. We also offer two pools, one specifically for children.
